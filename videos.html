Grouchy Mark Productions Video page

This is our main video page showcasing examples of the type of content we strive to create and produce.
We hope to be able to offer a wide array of produced videos while providing a one-stop shop — research and face-to-face meetings with clients to discover what they desire, whether piecing together a highlight reel to assist a student-athlete hoping to play at the next level or scanning a group of vintage photos to include in a video slideshow creation to honor a loved one who has passed on or a tribute piece to the celebrate noteworthy accomplishments of someone very derserving. Perhaps you need a 30-second theater ad or a two to three minute overview of your company or service— video can be a viable solution for online applications or promotional DVD's to hand out to prospective clients. We'll strive to do all we can to earn your business and trust, from meeting with you to gather your thoughts, ideas and objectives. From there, will apprise you of eacg step along the way, such as story-boarding, scripting, filming, editing and mastering the finished product, for whatever it may be utilized.

Promotional Video Above

The video above was produced as a promotional tool to attract nurses for a career at a local mental instution. With input from a staff committee, I set appointments with key personnel for on-camera interviews, prompting them with questions to answer regarding their thoughts and experiences at the facility. These video segments, along with some footage of the community, the facility and it's grounds were edited and pieced together for the finished production. There was more I intended to add to this piece, but sadly, it was axed.
